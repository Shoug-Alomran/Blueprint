# 02 · Discovery

Discovery defines everything that gets built. Goals, audience, content readiness, and required sections are confirmed here. Once discovery is complete, scope is locked.

---

## What Gets Defined

<div class="card-grid hero-cards">

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Goals & Audience</h3>
    <p>Who is this site for, and what must they understand within the first two minutes of browsing?</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Required Sections</h3>
    <p>Define all sections and confirm they fall within the included tier count.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Content Readiness</h3>
    <p>Confirm available copy, images, reports, and reference materials before build begins.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Launch Priorities</h3>
    <p>Identify what must be included in version one versus what can come later.</p>
  </article>

</div>

## Output of This Stage

- Agreed project scope and tier confirmation  
- Defined section list  
- Content inventory  
- Approved structural direction  

Scope is locked after this stage. Changes introduced after discovery is complete are assessed and quoted separately before implementation.
