# Discovery & Content Checklist

This stage defines scope, structure, and expectations before any design or build begins.

Clear discovery prevents rework later.

---

## What We Clarify

<div class="card-grid hero-cards">

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Goals & Audience</h3>
    <p>Who is this site for, and what must they understand quickly?</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Required Pages</h3>
    <p>Define core pages and navigation hierarchy.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Content Readiness</h3>
    <p>Confirm available copy, images, and references.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Launch Priorities</h3>
    <p>Identify what must be included in version one.</p>
  </article>

</div>

---

## What You Prepare

<div class="card-grid">

  <article class="info-card">

  </article>

  <article class="info-card">

    <p>Images, logos, diagrams, or supporting documents.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="info-card">

---

## Output of This Stage

- Agreed project scope  
- Defined page hierarchy  
- Content inventory  
- Approved structural direction  

Once discovery is complete, structure is locked and the build phase begins.

Structured discovery ensures efficient delivery.
