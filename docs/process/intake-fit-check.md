# 01 · Intake & Fit Check

No project begins without a confirmed fit. This stage ensures the correct tier is selected, expectations are aligned, and scope is realistic before any time is committed on either side.

---

## What Gets Confirmed

<div class="card-grid hero-cards">

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Project Type</h3>
    <p>Portfolio, project documentation, research documentation, or CV template. The correct tier is identified here.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Scope Fit</h3>
    <p>Confirm the project falls within static website scope. Dynamic systems, authentication, and databases fall outside Blueprint services.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Timeline Feasibility</h3>
    <p>Verify that the requested deadline is achievable given current availability and content readiness.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="hero-card">
    <h3>Add-On Requirements</h3>
    <p>Identify any add-ons needed upfront, including bilingual setup, custom domain, HTML report conversion, or priority delivery.</p>
  </article>

</div>

---

## What You Prepare

<div class="card-grid">

  <article class="info-card">

  </article>

## Output of This Stage

- Confirmed project tier  
- Agreed add-ons and total scope  
- Response within 2 business days  

Once intake is confirmed, the project moves to Discovery.
