# Contact

Use this page to determine project fit and begin the intake process.

Blueprint Studio works best when scope and expectations are clear from the start.

---

## Before You Reach Out

Please prepare the following:

- Project type (portfolio, documentation hub, workshop page, etc.)
- Estimated page count
- Preferred timeline
- Whether bilingual setup is needed
- Whether custom domain setup is required
- Current content readiness (complete / partial / not ready)

Clear preparation leads to faster responses and smoother kickoff.

---

## What Makes a Strong Inquiry

Strong inquiries typically include:

- Clear target audience  
- Defined purpose of the website  
- Confirmation that the project fits static website scope  
- Organized content (bio, CV, chapters, materials, etc.)  

If your project requires databases, authentication, or dynamic systems, Blueprint may not be the right fit.
