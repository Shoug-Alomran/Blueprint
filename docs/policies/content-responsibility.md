# Content Responsibility Policy

Clients are solely responsible for all content provided for their project.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Text and written materials  
- Images, graphics, and diagrams  
- Project descriptions and case details  
- Academic claims and research statements  
- External links and references  
- Logos, branding elements, and third-party assets  

Blueprint Studio provides structure and presentation services only. Content accuracy remains the responsibility of the client.

---

## Content Rights & Permissions

By submitting materials, the client confirms that:

- They own or have legal permission to use all provided content.  
- The content does not infringe copyright, trademark, or intellectual property rights.  
- The content does not contain plagiarism or misrepresentation.  
- The content complies with applicable laws and institutional policies.  

Blueprint Studio does not independently verify authorship, academic validity, or licensing rights.

---

## AI-Generated or Third-Party Content

If AI-generated content, stock images, templates, or third-party materials are used, the client is responsible for:

- Confirming usage rights  
- Ensuring compliance with licensing terms  
- Verifying originality and attribution requirements  

---

## Academic Integrity

For academic projects, the client is responsible for:

- Ensuring submission compliance with institutional rules  
- Confirming originality of research and written work  
- Meeting all academic honesty standards  

Blueprint Studio is not responsible for grading outcomes or institutional review decisions.

---

## Limitation of Liability

The Studio is not liable for:

- Legal disputes arising from submitted content  
- Intellectual property claims  
- Academic misconduct claims  
- Misrepresentation or factual inaccuracies  
- Consequences resulting from third-party use of provided materials  

All responsibility for content remains with the client.
